Fish Health

Our veterinarians hold more than 30 years of experience in the industry. Combining the knowledge from both the Norwegian and the Icelandic industry helps us to better understand the complexity of health matters in fish farming and how to prevent illness in the stock. We care about the industry and will work continuously to provide knowledge, training, and support to the growing industry. Together we can create welfare and vitality to the fish.
